About

Welcome to Angular, where we craft more than just pizza—we create an experience that brings people together. Established in 1997, our family-owned pizzeria is dedicated to quality, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques to make every pizza from scratch. From our hand-tossed crusts to our rich sauces, each bite bursts with flavor. Our diverse menu includes classic favorites like Margherita and Pepperoni, as well as unique creations like Pineapple'o'clock. We pride ourselves on providing excellent service in a warm, welcoming atmosphere, making every visit memorable. Whether you’re dining in, taking out, or ordering for delivery, we invite you to experience the best pizza in town at Angular Pizza.
